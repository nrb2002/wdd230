Site Plan

Site name

Kinshasa Chamber of Commerce

This name is linked to city of Kinshasa where the Chamber of Commerce operates.

Site purpose

We promote entrepreneurship and defend the rights of micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises operating in Kinshasa. We also advocate for an improved business environment while contributing to the local economy's health by increasing GDP and reducing unemployment through job creation..

We are therefore the platform par excellence that not only connects, leads, inspires, and advocates for communities of local entrepreneurs, but also helps them meet the challenges of innovation.

Scenarios

Once you become a member, you'll benefit from seamless sponsorship from our association. We advocate on behalf of our members with legal and state institutions to safeguard their business rights. Additionally, we'll pass on to you the unique business opportunities offered by our network of experienced entrepreneurs.

There are many sources of funding: our members, our association, the Fond de Promotion de l'Industrie (FPI), the Agence National de la Promotion de l'Industrie (ANAPI), the Fond de Garantie de l'Entrepreneuriat au Congo (FOGEC), the World Bank, the European Union, the African Development Bank (ADB), and many others.

We are not and do not represent any government agency. However, we work closely with the government to take part in the major decisions affecting the business climate in the Democratic Republic of Congo.
