Site plan

Site name

Kinshasa Chamber of Commerce

This name is linked to city of Kinshasa where the Chamber of Commerce operates.

Site purpose

We promote entrepreneurship and defend the rights of micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises operating in Kinshasa. We also advocate for an improved business environment while contributing to the local economy's health by increasing GDP and reducing unemployment through job creation..

We are therefore the platform par excellence that not only connects, leads, inspires, and advocates for communities of local entrepreneurs, but also helps them meet the challenges of innovation.
